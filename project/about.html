About Stay Prayed

Stay Prayed was founded by Madokuboba Dokubo with a vision to create a global online space for people to find encouragement, community, and spiritual strength through prayer.

Through our digital prayer groups, live events, and anonymous prayer requests, we aim to bring light into lives and faith into hearts all over the world.
